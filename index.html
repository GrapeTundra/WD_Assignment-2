Who are we?

Oscar's Recomended Cuisine Array, or ORCA for short is an array of resturants and dining locations that acts as a resturant discovery and reservation platform.
We aim to help spread the word of some good cuisine that we have personally gone to and tried, and want to give them the fame we belive they deserve

Our values

The values we search for in a resturant are:

  • Convenience: We don't want to have to jump through hoops to get our food, so if they use a website for ordering the page should be formatted well and easy to understand
  • Time to Taste Ratio: The longer the food takes to be made, the greater the expectations on the taste and overall quality are
  • Aesthetic: Does the aesthetic of the resturant match the cuisine and sort of enviroment such creates
  • Variety: We like it when a resturant has a large menu, with variety in ingredients allowing everyone in the group to be able to find something they like

Our Target Audience

We aim to help anyone who is struggling to find a resturant, whether a parent that doesn't feel like cooking, or maybe you're struggling to find a place for date night, no matter what we promise to try our best to alleviate that stress

Our Services

We provide a series of services such as:
A review page with our opinions on an array of resturants,
A recomendation page based on factors such as your dietry or budget restrictions,
Or even a reservation page to book a reservation for our array of resturants
